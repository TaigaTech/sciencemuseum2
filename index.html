About Us

The Scientific Museum inspires curiosity through immersive exhibitions, interactive labs, and live demos across physics, biology, and astronomy. Our mission is to make science accessible and exciting for all ages.

With over 50 interactive exhibits and state-of-the-art facilities, we provide a unique learning experience that combines education with entertainment.
